What are Bird Spikes?

Bird spikes are simple yet ingenious devices designed to discourage birds, especially pigeons, from landing or roosting on surfaces such as ledges, sills, beams, and rooftops. Typically made from durable materials like stainless steel, plastic, or polycarbonate, these spikes create an uncomfortable surface for birds to land on without causing them any harm. By eliminating potential roosting sites, bird spikes effectively persuade pigeons to move on to more welcoming locations.
